Research and Analysis - An optimization of a website

2017 路 SELF INITIATED 路 WEB

A whole array of problems were discovered, but all of them amendable. The whole research and analysis was accumulated into a UX presentational report, where I thoroughly describe the principles of the research and analysis as well as submitting my humble recommendations.
