Research and Analysis - An optimization of a website

2017 · SELF INITIATED · WEB

The point of this project was to build onto an already working website in the jobs and employment sphere and optimize it in every aspect.


This required various research methods and a disciplined UX mindset to produce efficient results so the website can not only compete and beat the difficult competition but to bring it's users an efficient way of finding / publishing job opportunities.


A whole array of problems were discovered, but all of them amendable. The whole research and analysis was accumulated into a UX presentational report, where I thoroughly describe the principles of the research and analysis as well as submitting my humble recommendations.
