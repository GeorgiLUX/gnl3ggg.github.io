Lifestyle for some, business for others

EasyForSure is a platform that makes life more bearable. It offers local services for individuals or groups targeted at expats and natives. Local businesses can also expand their online presence by signing on the platform. Various services are offered like sports, personal upkeep, restaurants, bars, social and cultural events.

Can you compensate qualitative research?

Learning about users without actually having to even interview them was quite challenging, I had to rely mostly on quantitative methods, desk  and competitive research. I compensated further with municipal records, social media research, and stakeholder interviews.
‍
Based on the data and insights, I began strategizing how to optimize engagement and effective communication between platform and users and platform and prospective business partners.

No type of research can compensate for the lack of another, each is tailored to extract specific data, qualitative research explores the individual appeal, models, views, and cannot be compensated for.

Social media research - lead generation using variables

How to reattune a platform

I created different content strategies based on available budget for platform exposure, lead generation and increased active users and revenue.
‍
The easiest strategy is to optimize the quality and quantity of media production - banners, ads, videos, posts, engaging with the community, etc.I created the marketing content via Canva, Adobe XD and other web tools.Other strategies were concerned with the UX copy - I wrote many versions of email templates for outreach to potential business partners. This new version changed the tone, explored emotional tones, and inserted keywords for brand value emphasis.

Next, a heuristic analysis was performed on the current platform to map out existing pain points, problems with engagement and usability. Due to limited time I could only redesign key site pages - concerning the main flow:
Visit site >  Select activity > Arrange activity

The value of marketing and copy for a designer

By the end the platform was refreshed from a usability and engagement perspective.
‍
Marketing production was supplied with 6 months content in advance. This project introduced me to research methods I hadn't explored till that point, I got to practice quantitative methods which aren't as often used by designers.

They provide great value especially when exploring collective groups. ESF also introduced me to digital platform design, something that would be of great use when I would take the leading design role at TechnologyCatalogue.

Practicing marketing and copywriting provided me with experience and intimate knowledge of the interconnected value that they bring to the UX equation, how they work together and why marketing is essential for digital platforms.
