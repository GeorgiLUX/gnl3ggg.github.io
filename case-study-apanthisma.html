Municipal Digitization

It's a great UX step to a better power structure...if you can afford it.

In this case, a median approach was selected with a centralizer app that groups all municipal apps in one convenient place for  use by residents.This solution offers great flexibility by empowering different groups with various advantages - personal or collective.

Night mode. Some of the apps are services that work 24/7,
optimal contextual use is a great steps to turning users into fans.

Universalism in design

Usability and content heuristics of existing apps helped in revealing the general vibe and brand voice for a G2C app.

The value of the app is as broad and lifeless as stone, the user group has no contextual angles to be approached from, so universalism in interaction is key.

Revealing the expression of universal UI components or interactions would be useful for future projects onwards.

Key UX Factors for this design.

Universalism in UX Factors

What is universalism in interaction? Deeply rooted repeating behavior defined by our physiological existence or molded by habit.
Easiest example is the placement of headers, logos, and certain buttons in IA's.
‍
While this was the smallest project of 7  I worked on during my internship, it helped flesh out my understanding of UI components for mobile, particularly which components  are universally usable.
