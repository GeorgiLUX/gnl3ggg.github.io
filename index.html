Hello, and welcome to my UX portfolio!



My name is George Lazarov and I'm an upcoming user experience designer. This online portfolio serves as an example of my website-building skills and overall programming skills. It contains my completed work for the moment as a freshman in the UX world. It's also a place where I can share my motivations and express my passion for UX.


The Nielsen-Norman Group once (or several times probably) said that "True User Experience goes beyond just giving customers what they say they want."
In that sense, I think being a UX designer is close to being a creator.
A creator who constructs a firm bridge between the way technology is used and the people who use this technology.
A creator whose chisel and hammer are empathy, logic, intuition and creativeness.
