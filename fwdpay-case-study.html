First steps in the startup world

The FWDPay team needed a UX designer for a first stage fintech startup. I was tasked with redesigning  the mobile app via material design with an accent on efficiency.
‍
I delved deep into business ownership, business models, lenses and patterns, fintech startups and material mobile design in order to understand the environment and goals.

This project introduced me to the huge amount of work going on in small startups and motivated me to pursue, later on, a course for business and tech innovation.

FWDPay's innovative value comes from offering digital group financial pockets with variation on the types and direction of use of payments. There is definitely potential of wide group use by in different industries.
